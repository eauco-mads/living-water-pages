6.0 pH — Aftercare
The Aftercare Water

pH 6.0 matches the skin's natural pH (4.5–6.5). Soothes inflammation, reduces redness, and supports healing without disrupting the skin's acid mantle. Build it into your aftercare protocol — it's something no other studio near you is doing.
