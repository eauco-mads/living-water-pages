Yes — and you'll taste it immediately. Water makes up 96–98.5% of a coffee drink. The pH, mineral content, and structure of your water directly affects extraction. Kangen water at 8.5–9.5 pH extracts more of the aromatic compounds and fewer of the harsh bitter notes. Most shops do a side-by-side the first day and notice it right away. Your regulars will too.
What is molecular hydrogen and why does it matter? +
Molecular hydrogen (H₂) is the smallest molecule in existence — it crosses into every cell in the body, including through the blood-brain barrier. It's a selective antioxidant that neutralizes the most harmful free radicals without disrupting beneficial processes. Over 1,000 peer-reviewed studies link it to reduced inflammation, faster recovery, and improved cognitive function. Clients who drink it daily notice the difference in how they feel — and they come back for it.
