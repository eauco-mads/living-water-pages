Why does pH 6.0 make hair feel so different? +
Hair's natural pH sits between 4.5 and 5.5. The cuticle — the outer protective layer of each strand — opens when exposed to alkaline water (like tap water at pH 7–8.5) and closes under mildly acidic conditions. Open cuticles mean frizz, porosity, dullness, and faster color fade. pH 6.0 beauty water closes the cuticle on contact. The result: smoother texture, more shine, better color retention, and less frizz — without adding a single product. Clients feel it before they leave the chair.
Is pH 2.5 actually safe to use at nail stations? +
Yes. Electrolyzed water at pH 2.5 contains hypochlorous acid (HOCl) — the same compound your immune system produces to kill pathogens. It's recognized by the FDA and EPA as safe and effective for surface disinfection, kills bacteria, fungi, and viruses in under 30 seconds, leaves no chemical residue, has no harmful fumes, and is safe for skin contact. It's already used in hospitals, dental offices, and food prep facilities. For a nail salon, it replaces quats and bleach-based sanitizers entirely — same or better kill rate, none of the chemical exposure for clients or techs.
What does the membership system actually look like at the counter? +
You put a small QR code placard near your reception desk or shampoo station. A client scans it, fills out a short form on their phone, and gets their free Water Share in about 30 seconds. They receive an automated email with their personalized digital member card. When they come back to fill up, a staff member types their name into the verification app and sees their tier color instantly. No spreadsheets, no manual tracking, no extra admin. The whole thing runs in the background while you focus on services.
What is molecular hydrogen and why would clients want it? +
Molecular hydrogen (H₂) is the smallest molecule in existence — small enough to cross into every cell, including through the blood-brain barrier. It acts as a selective antioxidant, neutralizing the most damaging free radicals without disrupting the ones your body needs. Over 1,000 peer-reviewed studies link it to reduced inflammation, faster recovery, improved cellular function, and better energy. In plain language: it tastes cleaner than anything out of a tap or a bottle, and the clients who drink it regularly report feeling the difference. That experience is what converts them to paid members.
