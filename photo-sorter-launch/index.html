1Founding 10 — reach out, get them in, get the testimonial
The message to send
"Hey! I just finished building something I've been working on for months. I'm inviting 10 founding users to test it before I officially launch. In exchange, I'd love your honest feedback and, if you love it, a testimonial I can use on my website."

Send it now, not after the copy is perfect — while they're testing, you're still editing. By the time they're done, you'll have real testimonials instead of hypothetical ones.

Once they're in

Follow up after they've had a day or two with it. Ask directly for a testimonial — specific beats vague. Show a range when you post them: one very specific review ("I do print/stock/travel/screenshots") next to one broad keep‑or‑delete one. "As specific or as loose as you want it" is a real differentiator worth a line in the FAQ once you have both kinds banked.
