The science

Why it works.

1,000+ peer-reviewed studies. ISO 13485 medical device certified. The questions your clients will ask — answered.

What exactly is this water? +
Electrolyzed Reduced Water (ERW) — structured through electrolysis, which splits water into an alkaline stream (drinking) and an acidic stream (skin, cleaning). The drinking water becomes rich in molecular hydrogen (H₂) with a negative ORP that donates electrons and neutralizes free radicals. The Kangen K8 holds ISO 13485 medical device certification. Over 1,000 peer-reviewed studies support the mechanism.

Read the full science →
What does molecular hydrogen do for the body? +
Molecular hydrogen (H₂) is the smallest molecule in existence — it crosses into every cell, including through the blood-brain barrier. It selectively neutralizes the most harmful free radicals without disrupting beneficial oxidative processes. Peer-reviewed studies link it to reduced inflammation, faster recovery, improved cognitive function, and cellular protection. For clients post-procedure or post-injection, the anti-inflammatory effect is especially relevant.

See the studies →
Why is pH 6.0 good for skin and hair? +
Healthy skin has a natural pH between 4.5 and 6.5. Most tap water is 7.0–8.5, which disrupts the skin's acid mantle and increases sensitivity and dryness. pH 6.0 beauty water matches the skin's environment — it tightens pores, soothes inflammation, and helps the skin rebalance without additives. For hair, it closes the cuticle, adds shine, and reduces frizz. Clients notice it before they leave your chair.
Is the pH 2.5 sanitizer safe? +
Yes. Electrolyzed oxidizing water at pH 2.5 (hypochlorous acid) is recognized by the FDA and EPA as safe and effective for surface sanitation. Used in hospitals, dental offices, and food processing. Unlike bleach, it leaves no chemical residue, has no fumes, and is safe for skin contact. Works on contact in under 30 seconds and reverts to regular water after use.
